Welcome to Snek!

This game is developed by: Stefan Aldus

The goal of the game is for the "snake" (the small black dot) to "eat" the apples, to do this, move with the W/A/S/D or arrow keys.

Each time you eat an apple, your "snake" gets faster, if you hit the sides of the play area, you have died!

At any point in the game, you may press your "ESC" key to exit back to this menu, however the game will be reset!
